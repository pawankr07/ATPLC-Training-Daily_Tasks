Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts

In case of emergency, please contact:

  • 911 - Emergency Services
  • 555-1234 - Local Police Department
  • 555-5678 - Fire Department

Emergency Preparedness

It's important to be prepared for emergencies. Here are some tips:

  • Create a family emergency plan
  • Prepare an emergency kit with supplies such as food, water, and first aid
  • Stay informed about local emergency situations and follow instructions from authorities

Emergency Alerts

Stay informed about emergency situations in our district:

  • Sign up for our emergency notification system to receive alerts via text, email, or phone call
  • Follow our social media accounts for updates during emergencies
  • Listen to local news and radio stations for updates

Emergency Safety

During an emergency, it's important to prioritize your safety. Here are some tips:

  • Stay calm and try to remain as still as possible to avoid injury
  • Follow instructions from emergency responders and other authorities
  • If you are in a building, seek shelter in a safe location such as a basement or interior room
  • If you are outdoors, move to a low-lying area and avoid trees and other tall objects
