About Us

We are the district government responsible for managing and maintaining the infrastructure, public services, and resources for the residents and businesses within our district.

Our mission is to provide safe, clean, and sustainable environments for our community to live, work, and thrive in. We strive to uphold transparency, accountability, and responsiveness in all our operations.

Explore our website to learn more about the services we offer, events we host, and resources available to our community. Contact us if you have any questions or feedback.
